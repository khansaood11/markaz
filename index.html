About Darul Uloom Markaz-e-Islami

Introduction of Madressa

Ankleshwar Markaz-e-Islami Trust, is one of the most celebrated Islamic institutes in Gujarat, India, catering and imparting Islamic Education to the Muslim students at no cost. The foundation stone of Darul Uloom Markaz-e-Islami was laid by two distinguished and remarkable personalities of their time i.e. Maulana Raza Ahmad Ajmeri (RA) and Hazrat Maulana Muinuddin Sahab (Khalifa Hazrat Shaikhul Hadith) in 1984 A.C. corresponding to 1405 A.H. Now while those charismatic persons have passed away, Darul Uloom is working under the able patronage of a renowned and well-known personality Hazrat Maulana Qamruzzaman Saheb Allahabadi. Al-hamdu Lillah, it is heading towards glorious success by spreading multiple faculties.

Markaz-e-Islami

Darul Uloom caters Alimyat Course (Graduation in Islamic Studies) to the students from Urdu grade to Final, Daurah-e-Hadith, on the pattern of the famous Islamic seminary Darul Uloom, Deoband and other eminent Islamic institutions in India adhering to Dars-e-Nizami with meager changes, sensing the need of time, such as introducing select computer programmes and English Language up to the fourth grade students of Arabic classes. There are 10 Hifz classes wherein about 225 students are guided to memorize the Glorious Qur’an under the strict supervision of expert teachers.

Markaz-e-Islami also opened a faculty in 2003 named “Markaz-e-Islami Education and Research Centre”, introducing a 2-years special English course. It aims to equip Madrasa Graduates (Ulama) with English Language and modern technology, like computer and internet, with the intention that they could find themselves capable to represent Islam and true teachings in proper way, in this modern world to the so-called modern educated people. It has been, so far, very successful and the faculty has produced seven batches comprising 98 Ulama and at present 47 Ulama are under training in first and final years. It is needless to mention here that it is part of the curriculum of the department for the students to join Tablighi Jam’aah once a year for forty days as a practical training.

One primary school, named ‘Markaz-e-Islami Priamary School’, is also run under the management of Darul Uloom Markaz-e-Islami recognized by Gujarat Government in the city, Ankleshwar. There are approximately 356 students admitted in different classes ranging from KG to 8th grade.

Al-Hamdu Lillah, Darul Uloom Markaz-e-Islami, so far, has produced 245 Ulama, 196 Qaris and 268 Hafize Quran and 75 Madrasa Graduates equipped with English language and modern tools. Darul Uloom Markaz-e-Islami, Ankleshwar, in the current year has enrolled 450 students in different sections. There are 33 teachers offering their yeomen services to the institution and 15 other attendants are at service in Darul Uloom Markaz-e-Islami.

It is a bird’s eye view of Darul Uloom Markaz-e-Islami and you are always welcome to visit us. May the institution stride ahead by leaps and bounds with your sincere prayer and cooperation! May accept the service of this noble institution!

Admission

Madrasa Admission Rules

  • Eligibility: Applicants must be aged 10 or above with basic reading skills and a commitment to Islamic values.
  • Dress Code: Students must wear traditional Islamic attire (kurta-pajama, topi) during classes and prayers.
  • Attendance: 100% attendance is mandatory for all classes and daily prayers unless excused by the administration.
  • Mobile Phones: Use of mobile phones is prohibited during classes and study hours to maintain focus.
  • Conduct: Students must adhere to Islamic etiquette, respect teachers, and maintain discipline at all times.
  • Residence: Residential students must follow hostel rules, including fixed meal and sleep schedules.

Donate

Darul Uloom Markaze Islami’s various charitable activities are financed by contributions given by generous people

Darul Uloom Markaze Islami accepts all sorts of contributions that are appropriate to its mission such as all sorts of donations, Zakat (Islamic alms), Waqf (trusts), Sadaqa, grants, gifts, and bequests made for its favor.

Darul Uloom Markaze Islami also accepts designated contributions for specific purposes such as: sponsoring orphans or a specific project such as building mosques, health clinic, drilling a well, and the like.

The money given to us in charity is used for the specific purpose in case the donor specifies the purpose such as building a mosque and Madrasah or digging the borehole or feeding the children etc. or else it will be used wherever it is most needed for the cause of Darul Uloom Markaze Islami if purpose not specified.
